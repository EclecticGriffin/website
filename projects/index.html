Simulation and Debugging in the Calyx Ecosystem

Calyx is an open-source intermediate language for developing custom hardware accelerators. I built a custom interpreter and gdb-like debugger for the language, called Cider. My more recent work has focused on extending this tool further with additional analyses to detect hardware-specific bugs throughout the Calyx ecosystem.

Use-based Privacy

Use-based privacy is an approach which protects data based on how it is used, rather than just who can access it. For instance, you may wish to share your location with your co-workers, but only in the form of what room you are in within your workplace and only during the workday. These types of rich privacy policies can be implemented using systems such as Ancile!
